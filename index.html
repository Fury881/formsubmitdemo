Orthotics

Orthotics are the correction of disorders of the limbs or spine by use of braces and other devices to correct alignment or provide support. Wright Brace & Limb can get an off-the-shelf orthosis or make a custom orthosis made by professionals.

Prosthetics

Prosthetics are an artifical body part, such as a leg, arm, etc. Wright Brace & Limb can get off-the-shelf Prosthetics that can fit any body part in a wide range of sizes, or Wright Brace & Limb can make a custom prosthesis made by professionals.
